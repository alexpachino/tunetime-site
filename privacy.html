Privacy Policy

Last updated March 2026

Overview

Tune Time is designed with privacy in mind. The app does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Microphone Access

Tune Time requests access to your device's microphone solely to power the chromatic tuner feature. Audio is processed entirely on-device in real time and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted anywhere.

Data Collection

Tune Time does not collect any personal information, usage data, analytics, or crash reports. No data is sent to any server — there are no servers.

Third-Party Services

Tune Time does not integrate any third-party SDKs, advertising networks, or analytics services.

Children's Privacy

Tune Time does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date.
